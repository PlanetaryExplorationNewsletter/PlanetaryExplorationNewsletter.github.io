NASA DUE DATES DELAYED DUE TO STORM

The Due Dates for Maturation of Instruments for Solar System 
Exploration, Nancy Grace Roman Technology Fellowships in Astrophysics, 
and Outer Planets Research have all been delayed by a week to give more 
time to proposers who will be without power because of Hurricane Sandy.

The new due date for proposals to Maturation of Instruments for Solar 
System Exploration is November 7, 2012.

The new due date for proposals to Nancy Grace Roman Technology 
Fellowships in Astrophysics is November 8, 2012.

The new due date for proposals to Outer Planets Research is November 9, 
2012.

Questions concerning MatISSE may be directed to Janice Buckner at
janice.l.buckner@nasa.gov or 202-358-0183

Questions concerning Astro Tech Fellows may be directed to Louis
Kaluzienski at Louis.J.Kaluzienski@nasa.gov or 202-358-0365

Questions concerning OPR may be directed to Terry Hurford at
terry.a.hurford@nasa.gov or 301-286-4249

Also, the due date for applications to the NASA Postdoctoral Program
is shifted from November 1 to November 5, 2012. For further information
go to:

http://nasa.orau.org/postdoc/
