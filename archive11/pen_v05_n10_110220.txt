[NASA] 2ND EDUCATION OPPORTUNITIES IN NASA STEM (EONS) WORKSHOP

February 23, 2011
NASA Marshall Institute
US Space and Rocket Center
1 Tranquility Base
Huntsville, Alabama 35805
Registration not required
 
The NASA Office of Education is accepting proposals under the Education 
Opportunities in NASA STEM (EONS) NASA Research Announcement. 
This NEW announcement is an umbrella announcement for opportunities 
under the Minority University Research and Education Program (MUREP), 
and includes calls for proposals in the following program elements for 
Fiscal Year 2011:

    - Curriculum Improvement Partnership Award for the Integration 
       of Research into the Undergraduate Curriculum (CIPAIR)
    - Innovations in Global Climate Change Education (IGCCE)
    - MUREP Small Projects (MSP)
    - Marshall Space Flight Center (MSFC) Center Unique Projects
    - Stennis Space Center (SSC) Center Unique Projects 
  
On site registration begins at 8:00 a.m CST.  Participation is not 
required to submit a proposal.  For more information regarding this 
opportunity, please visit the EONS page on the NSPIRES website. Go to:

http://nspires.nasaprs.com

(click on Solicitations, then on Open Solicitations)
 
This workshop will include a networking session to give institutions 
and organizations an opportunity to collaborate.
 
If you have additional questions concerning the workshop please email: 
NASAEONS2011@nasaprs.com

THE EUROPEAN SPACE AGENCY (ESTEC, THE NETHERLANDS) RECRUITS A SCIENTIST

A selection of duties and responsibilities:
- providing scientific support to future ESA robotic exploration 
   mission studies to Mars;
- providing scientific expertise to the presently ongoing 
   implementation of the ExoMars Programme;
- performing scientific research, preferably in the area of 
   terrestrial planets, in particular Mars.

Qualifications:
PhD or equivalent degree in the field of Planetary Sciences
Substantial experience in particular utilizing space-based 
instrumentation.
Scientific areas: geology, geophysics, geochemistry, exobiology and
atmospheric sciences

Applications are only considered from nationals of one of the 
following States: Austria, Belgium, the Czech Republic, Denmark, 
Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Ireland, Italy, Luxembourg, the 
Netherlands, Norway, Portugal, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, the 
United Kingdom and Canada.

More at:

http://www.esa.int/hr/PDF/ESA-VN-ESTEC-2010-082-REV.1.pdf

Deadline: 14 March 2011
