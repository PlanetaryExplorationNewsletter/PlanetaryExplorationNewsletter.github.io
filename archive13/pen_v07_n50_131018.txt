[NASA] NEW PROPOSAL DUE DATES FOR MDAP, PMDAP, OPR, NAI, MARS 2020

During the Government shutdown, the proposal due dates for MDAP and
PMDAP were set to TBD in order for Government proposers to have time 
to prepare their submissions. Now that the Government has resumed work, 
new due dates have been set for these programs. In addition, new 
proposal due dates have been set for other programs (OPR) with an 
original due date in the near future. Updated due dates are as follows:

Mars Data Analysis Program (MDAP): Wednesday, October 23, 2013

Planetary Mission Analysis Program (PMDAP): Monday, October 28, 2013

Outer Planets Research (OPR): Friday, November 15, 2013

NAI Cycle 7 CAN Step 1: Monday, November 18, 2013

NAI Cycle 7 CAN Step 2: Wednesday, February 19, 2014

Mars 2020 Pre-Proposal Conference: Monday, October 28, 2013

Mars 2020 NOI: Monday, November 4, 2013

Mars 2020 Proposal Due Date: Wednesday, January 15, 2014
